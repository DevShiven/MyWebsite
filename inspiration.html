Inspiration

I'm constantly inspired by the work of legendary studios like id Software (creators of DOOM, Wolfenstein and Quake), Bethesda Studios (creators of Fallout and The Elder Scrolls), Valve (the creators of legendary games like Half-Life, Portal, Team Fortress and Counter Strike), for their amazing story-telling elements and addictive gameplay, and independent developers like Toby Fox (creator of Undertale and Deltarune). Their innovative approaches to game design fuel my own creative fire.
